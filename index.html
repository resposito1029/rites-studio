mission

Despite unprecedented levels of global interconnectedness, humanity has never been lonelier. We seem to have lost sight of what brings us joy and fulfillment. So caught up in presenting a perfect image, we often forget the purpose of our gatherings—the rites of human existence.

The ceremonies, rituals and spaces we invite others into have become commonplace and uninspired. We can change that together.

We transform spaces into sites for reflection, profound emotion, and shared human experience. Our work is intimate and small but leaves you with a feeling of something greater. We make places of pause, not of passing fascination. Places of gravity that bring together humanity.

Our process is rooted in fluid collaboration, adaptability, and experimentation, ensuring that every Rites Studio installation is a unique and responsive creation, born of its place. We are driven by a mission of mindfulness—to create work that enhances, not detracts from the community it's in.

— our approach

mindfulness We are driven by a mission of mindfulness—to create work that enhances, not detracts from the community it's in. We accomplish these goals by being inclusive, creative and unyielding in our mission.
collaboration Our process is rooted in fluid collaboration and adaptability. We work with communities, creatives and clients to shape spaces that respond to the real needs of the place and the people in it.
experimentation Every Rites Studio installation is a unique and responsive creation, born of its place—never a template, always an experiment. We embrace failure as part of the process.
inclusivity Great public space belongs to everyone. We design for the full range of human experience, not for an ideal user. Our installations invite participation from anyone who encounters them.
